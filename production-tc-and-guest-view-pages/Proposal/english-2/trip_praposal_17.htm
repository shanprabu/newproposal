Apply for your visa

For entry to Cambodia you will need a visa. You can apply for your e-visa in advance by visiting the following website: https://www.evisa.gov.kh/. Please note that the e-visa is only valid for entry to Cambodia by air. When applying on line you will need: your passport which must have validity of more than six months balance at time of entry to Cambodia, a recent passport-size photo in digital format (JPEG or PNG format), a valid credit card (Visa/MasterCard). The visa fee is US$30 and the processing fee is US$7, so a total of US $37. After receiving your Visa through email, print it out and bring it along when you travel to Cambodia. Alternatively you can obtain your visa on arrival. You need to carry a copy of your passport and 2 passport size photographs. The visa fee is US$30.
