  Included Activities

Full Day Tour of Angkor Wat, Angkor Thom & Ta Prohm
Explore one of the seven wonders of the world with a fascinating full day tour of the glorious, ancient and impressive Angkor Wat. This stunning temple complex boasts five magnificent towers and vast gallery complex, with the main attraction being the dominating Angkor Wat, which magically reflects in nearby water at sunrise and sunset. Head onwards to Angkor Thom, a moated royal city in the bayon style of large faces on all sides. Discover Ta Phrom, a quiet and sprawling jungle clad monastic complex engulfed by massive fig and silk cotton trees. With incredible legend of regal feuds and religious overhaul, this temple complex is undoubtedly one of the finest in the world. For this reason, it can sometimes become busy with crowds.

Half Day Visit to Angkor National Museum
Gain an in-depth view into the origins of Khmer art, culture and architecture, with a half day visit to Angkor National Museum. Located in the heart of Siem Reap in a restored colonial building, this specialist archaeological museum was only built in 2007, so you can expect state-of-the-art multimedia technology that brings all the exhibitions to life. Throughout the eight galleries you can experience this comprehensive collection of Cambodian relics, which highlight the history of the Khmer Empire, and also various Buddhist and Hindu antique artifacts. Special exhibitions and events take place throughout the year.

Tonle Sap Lake Boat Ride
Drive out of the city for about 45 minutes into the countryside, for a unique lake-living experience! Stop off on the way at a local village. Arriving at the quay you will board a local boat that will take you on a 1.5 hour cruise of Tonle Sap Lake, known as the Great Lake of Cambodia, and home to the unique floating villages. Discover how communities live and work on the lake - with floating schools, churches, stores and markets! You can observe how crocodiles farming forms an important part of this culture. This half day experience is best enjoyed from July to February when water levels are high.

Apply for your visa

Peru: No visa is required for travel to Peru for US, UK, Canadian, Australian and European Union citizens. If you are from another country, you may visit the nearest consulate of the country you are travelling to obtain a tourist visa for travel to any of these countries if required.

Ecuador: No visa is required for travel to Ecuador for US, UK, Canadian, Australian and European Union citizens. If you are from another country, you may visit the nearest consulate of the country you are travelling to obtain a tourist visa for travel to any of these countries if required.

Bolivia: Visitors from the USA require a visa to travel to Bolivia, which can either be obtained prior to arrival in South America from the closest Bolivian consulate or completed at immigration upon arrival only at the international airports (La Paz / Santa Cruz). If you are entering Bolivia via land, you must obtain your visa prior to travel as most border posts are unable to issue visas.

India: Travelers from most countries will need a tourist visa to India, which can be obtained from the Indian embassy in your country of residence or. U.S., Canadian and Australian citizens may also register for a "visa on arrival" online under https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/ (for stays up to 30 days). Please take care of this well in advance, as embassies in some countries may not be able to rush visa applications. Important: Please note that a permit is required for guests visiting Sikkim and we suggest that you apply for this at the same time as your visa. This permit incurs an additional fee. Further information about how and where to apply can be found at: http://sikkim.nic.in/homedept/ilpfaqs.htm. All hotels in Darjeeling & Sikkim require a passport size photograph of you at the time of check in.

Cambodia: For entry to Cambodia you will need a visa. You can apply for your e-visa in advance by visiting the following website: https://www.evisa.gov.kh/. Please note that the e-visa is only valid for entry to Cambodia by air. When applying on line you will need: your passport which must have validity of more than six months balance at time of entry to Cambodia, a recent passport-size photo in digital format (JPEG or PNG format), a valid credit card (Visa/MasterCard). The visa fee is US$30 and the processing fee is US$7, so a total of US $37. After receiving your Visa through email, print it out and bring it along when you travel to Cambodia. Alternatively you can obtain your visa on arrival. You need to carry a copy of your passport and 2 passport size photographs. The visa fee is US$30.
