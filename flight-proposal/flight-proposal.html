Important Flight Information

Prices

The prices listed above are the prices as of today and they are subject to change. All fares are per person, including taxes.

Reserving Flights

Depending on the airline and fare type we can reserve some flights until you finalize the details of your trip. If you want us to reserve them for you, please let us know and we will send to you a booking form in which you will be required to enter your full name and passport details - these are required to hold this booking for you.

Booking Flights

Before we can book international flights for you, we will require:
  • A passport copy of each passenger travelling; and
  • Payment of the international flight cost in full

If you choose to book these flights, we will be happy to request specific seats based on your preference (subject to availability).

We can also provide your frequent flyer number to the airline if you provide it to us.
