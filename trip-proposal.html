Important Information

Important Notes...

Baggage allowance in India for international flights varies depending upon which airline and class you are traveling with. Kindly note that your domestic flight baggage allowance is usually less. As a rule of thumb, most airlines in India have a maximum baggage allowance of 15 kg (33 pounds) and you may be subject to excess weight baggage charges if you exceed this.

Don’t Forget To...

Apply for your visa
Travelers from most countries will need a tourist visa to India, which can be obtained from the Indian Embassy in your country of residence. Please take care of this well in advance, as embassies in some countries may not be able to rush visa applications. Important: Please note that a permit is required for guests visiting Sikkim and we suggest that you apply for this at the same time as your visa. This permit incurs an additional fee of USD $30. Further information about how and where to apply can be found at: http://sikkim.nic.in/homedept/ilpfaqs.htm All hotels in Darjeeling & Sikkim require a passport size photograph of you at the time of check in.

Purchase travel insurance
We highly encourage you to purchase travel insurance for your trip in order to guard against incurring a loss of your trip cost in case of cancellation caused due to medical illness, family emergency, personal reasons or other factors beyond one’s control.
